About

After a successful run at the races, Unusual Heatwave retires from the racetrack to start the next leg of his journey as a stallion. Beginning at the start of 2015, Unusual Heatwave will be standing at the illustrious Tommy Town Thoroughbreds in Santa Ynez, California.

Trained by Alexis Barba for Teresa McWilliams and Montecito Stables, Unusual Heatwave is a Three-Time Stakes winner and a 3 Year Old Cal-Bred Champion with earnings of $488,752. Defeating Rousing Sermon in the Real Good Deal Stakes, Unusual Heatwave also successfully took 1st in the Snow Chief Stake, carrying on his half-brother's legacy Alphie's Bet, and later the Crystal Water Stakes.

Out of Cand's Gold's mare, Miss Alphie and California Leading Sire Unusual Heat, Unusual Heatwave won at distances ranging from 6 1/2 Furlongs to 1 1/8 Miles on Turf and Synthetic.

2015 Stud Fee is $2,500 for a Live Foal, you can fill out the contact form below or you can also contact Mike Allen of Tommy Town Thoroughbreds at (805) 686-4337 or email info@tommytownfarms.com
